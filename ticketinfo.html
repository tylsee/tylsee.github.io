The GA Ticket includes:

  • Allows entrance to the venue
  • Food & dessert for purchase from award-winning chefs & restaurants including vegan, vegetarian & gluten-free options
  • Shop official band merch & festival merch
  • Sponsor areas with giveaways
  • Bars, concessions and specialty cocktail lounges
  • Free water stations
  • Secure lockers with charging capabilities for rent
  • Children 8 & under allowed in free with access to Kidzapalooza with a ticketed adult

The GA+ Ticket includes:

  • Unlimited access to two GA+ Lounges with relaxed seating* and air-conditioned restrooms (private groves near Buckingham Fountain)
  • Private bar with drinks for purchase with preferred pricing on all beverages
  • Dedicated food for purchase
  • Jumbo screens with livestreams from main stages

The VIP Ticket includes all the amenities of GA along with:

  • Dedicated on-field viewing* at both North and South Main Stages, just behind Platinum Viewing
  • Unlimited access to two VIP Lolla Lounges featuring relaxed seating, shade, and air-conditioned restrooms plus direct access to the on-field viewing areas
  • Private bar with drinks for purchase in both VIP Lounges
  • Dedicated food for purchase in both VIP Lounges
  • Complimentary water refill station in both VIP Lounges
  • Golf cart shuttle transportation between Lolla Lounges North and South
  • Jumbo screen in Lolla Lounge North with live streaming from the main stages
  • Complimentary festival hair & glitter services, lawn games, and more
  • Lockers and mobile charging units for rent (available first-come, first-served)
  • Dedicated VIP Concierge to assist with your festival needs
  • Dedicated entrance into festival
